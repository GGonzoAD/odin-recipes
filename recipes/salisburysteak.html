Salisbury Steak

Description

To make Salisbury Steak with Mushroom Gravy it is a filling comfort food

You grate the onion and the soak the breadcrumbs into the juices. You then sear the beef patties.

  • 1 Garlic Clove

  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tsp dijon mustard
  • Gravy
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/2 onion finely chopped
  • 5 oz mushrooms sliced
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp flour all purpose
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Saly and Pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1: Grate the Onion
  2. Step 2: Soak the breadcrumbs
  3. Step 3: Sear the beef patties
  4. Step 4: Then finish cooking them in the gravy.
