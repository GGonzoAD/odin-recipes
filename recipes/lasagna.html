Lasagna

Description

To make lasagna you first lay down a layer of meat sauce to a lightly greased casserole dish. Then you add a layer of boiled lasagna noodles which you then add a layer of ricotta cheese. You then add layer of meat sauce you repeat this process for two more times following the same layers.

You then top the lasagna with cheese usually mozzarella cheese. You then cover and bake at 375 for 30 minutes. Then take off the cover and bake for a further 15 minutes. You could optionaly broil it. You then let the Lasagna rest for 45 minutes.

Ingredients

    Ricotta cheese
