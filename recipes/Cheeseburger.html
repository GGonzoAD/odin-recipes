Cheeseburgers

Description

To make cheeseburgers

Ingredients

  • 1.2 lbs beef mince for single patties
  • 2.4 lbs beef mince
  • 1/8th tsp each salt and papper per burger
  • 2-3 tbsp canola oil
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4-8 american cheese slices
  • 8 romaine lettuce leaves
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 large gherkins

Steps

  1. Step 1: Separate the patties into 4 or 8 equal portions
  2. Step 2: Toast the buns
  3. Step 3: Cook the patties for 1 1/2 on each side
  4. Step 4: Put the burgers to rest
  5. Step 5: Put together the burgers and anything you want on it.
